Operation

Setting up Locked is incredibly quick and easy

Simply turn on and place on a door

30 seconds after the power button is pressed, Locked will activate, indicated by several flashes of its front light. In its default mode it will measure it's current heading and activate if it deviates by at least 10 degrees. This involve sounding a local alarm as well as sending you a text message.

Thanks to simply designed hooks, attaching Locked to any door is made easy with no extra setup required. You need only hang it over the back of a door.
